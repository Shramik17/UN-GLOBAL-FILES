CPLC Singapore

Who We Are

We are the first official chapter of the World Bank's Carbon Pricing Leadership Coalition (CPLC), jointly launched by GCNS and World Bank's CPLC

What We Do

Facilitate dialogue, knowledge sharing, and collaborations on carbon management, particularly internal carbon  pricing (ICP)

Why We Do

Our main philosophy: to help companies realise the carbon cost of their actions, and make conscious decisions to mitigate emissions

How CPLC Singapore Can Provide Value

Tools, resources, and training

(E.g our in-house Carbon & Emissions Recording Tool, carbon management workshops, as well as reports from the World Bank and United Nations on carbon pricing) 

Competitive advantage

From appealing to increasingly environmentally conscious customers and investors requiring more transparency 

Safe space

To explore carbon management and the ICP landscape, learn best practices , raise concerns or challenges, and grow in competence in implementing carbon offsetting or ICP as a potential method of meeting your sustainability targets

Networking opportunities

With like-minded profesionals/companies

Brand recognition

From showcasing companies' ICP efforts on regional and global platforms
