As the need for climate action grows in urgency globally, businesses need to stay relevant in the business ecosystem by offering business models and processes that are sensitive to climate change impact.

CERT has been developed to simplify the recording and management of emissions data, and enable companies to mitigate their climate impact while reducing  operational costs and risks.

(Please kindly note: the online CERT is not in operation at the moment as it is going through some upgrading works. For companies interested in accessing the CERT, this will be provided to companies participating in the LowCarbonSG programme.)

Carbon & Emissions Recording Tool

Curious about monitoring your carbon footprint but not sure where to start?

CERT is a guiding tool for companies to measure and monitor their emissions, paving the way for businesses to lower their carbon impact, in line with Singapore's decarbonisation agenda.
