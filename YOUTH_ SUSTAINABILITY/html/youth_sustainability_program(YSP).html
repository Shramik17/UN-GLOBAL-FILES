Youth Sustainability Program

What is YSP?

The Youth Sustainability Programme (YSP) is a youth initiative by GCNS todevelop our youth talent pool to meet the increase in green job demands andcreate career prospects in the area of sustainability.

Programme Overview & Details

Participants will be equipped with valuable career skills through our foundation course, along with potential 12-week-internship placements at companies with sustainability initiatives.

Topics covered in YSP include

  • Introduction to Corporate Sustainability and Sustainability Context
  • Introduction to Greenhouse Gases
  • Sustainability Reporting Frameworks
  • Overview of Sustainability Report Writing Process and Case Study

Programme Requirements:

  • The training workshop is open to all youths of age 18 to 35, based in Singapore
  • Certificate will only be given to participants who have completed all modules (internship is not required for certificate)
  • Potential internship opportunities are only offered to tertiary students/fresh graduates, based in Singapore (local or permanent residents)

Note

  • In order to be eligible for potential internship opportunities, eligible participants must complete the full training workshop.
  • GCNS does not guarantee any internship placement to the participants at the end of this programme.
  • Participants must submit a resume in order to participate in the internship selection process.
  • GCNS will match eligible participants to the available number of internship slots based on its best ability (up to 3 internship interviews).
  • Matching of eligible participants to internship interviews can take up to 2 months, starting from the last day of the training workshop.
  • There is a minimum of 20 participants required to confirm the programme.
