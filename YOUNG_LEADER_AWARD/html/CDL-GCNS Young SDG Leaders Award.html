What is CDL-GCNS Young SDG Leaders Award?

The CDL-GCNS Young SDG Leaders Award (YSDGLA) is a business case competition that provides youths with a platform to implement the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) within real-life corporate scenarios.

Jointly organised by City Developments Limited (CDL) and GCNS, the YSDGLA seeks to:

• Educate and empower young leaders to integrate the SDGs into business strategies and operations
• Provide businesses with innovative ideas on conducting responsible business
• Mobilise youths and the private sector towards advancing the SDGs

Since its inception in 2011, the case competition has nurtured more than 2,400 young leaders.

Think you have what it takes to shape the world of tomorrow? Take part in our 2021 case competition and stand a chance to win cash prizes and invaluable business attachment experiences.
